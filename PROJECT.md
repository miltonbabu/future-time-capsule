# Future Time Capsule

## English

**Future Time Capsule** is an AI-powered web application that generates personalized "newspapers from the future" for users. It turns your dreams and aspirations into beautifully designed vintage newspaper front pages, making the future tangible today.

### Features
- **AI Journalist**: Uses GLM-4-Flash to write engaging front-page articles about your future achievements
- **AI Illustrator**: Generates custom illustrations using CogView-3-Flash based on your achievement context
- **Vintage Design**: Authentic newspaper aesthetic with sepia tones, typewriter fonts, and 3D embossed effects
- **Bilingual Support**: Full English and Chinese language support
- **Shareable**: Generate unique share links and QR codes to share your future newspaper
- **Downloadable**: Export your newspaper as a high-quality PNG image
- **Card View**: WeChat Moments-style card view for social sharing

### Tech Stack
- Next.js 16 with App Router
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS 4
- GLM-4-Flash (article generation)
- CogView-3-Flash (image generation)
- Vercel deployment

### How It Works
1. Enter your name, team, and future achievement
2. Choose a future date and upload a photo
3. Our AI journalist writes your story
4. Our AI illustrator creates a matching scene
5. Share or download your personalized newspaper

## 中文

**未来时光胶囊**是一款由AI驱动的网页应用，为用户生成个性化的"来自未来的报纸"。它将你的梦想和抱负转化为设计精美的复古报纸头版，让未来在今天变得触手可及。

### 功能特点
- **AI记者**：使用GLM-4-Flash撰写关于你未来成就的精彩头版文章
- **AI插画师**：基于你的成就背景，使用CogView-3-Flash生成定制插图
- **复古设计**：正宗的报纸美学，带有复古色调、打字机字体和3D浮雕效果
- **双语支持**：完整的中英文语言支持
- **分享功能**：生成独特的分享链接和二维码，分享你的未来报纸
- **下载功能**：将报纸导出为高质量PNG图片
- **卡片视图**：微信朋友圈风格的卡片视图，适合社交分享

### 技术栈
- Next.js 16（App Router）
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS 4
- GLM-4-Flash（文章生成）
- CogView-3-Flash（图片生成）
- Vercel部署

### 使用流程
1. 输入你的姓名、团队和未来成就
2. 选择未来日期并上传照片
3. AI记者撰写你的故事
4. AI插画师创作匹配场景
5. 分享或下载你的个性化报纸
